TECHFLOW REFUND POLICY (2025)

1. Standard Returns:
   - Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
   - The customer must pay for return shipping unless the item is defective.

2. Defective Items:
   - If an item is damaged on arrival, TechFlow provides a full refund + free return label.
   - Proof of damage (photo) is required within 48 hours.

3. Manager Overrides (MANAGER ONLY):
   - Managers can approve refunds up to 90 days for "Loyal Customers".
   - Managers can waive shipping fees at their discretion to prevent bad reviews.

4. Non-Refundable:
   - Software licenses, Gift Cards, and "Final Sale" items cannot be returned.
